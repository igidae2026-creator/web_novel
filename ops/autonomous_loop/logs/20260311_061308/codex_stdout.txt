**Result**

Bottleneck: the prose readability gate only punished long sentences and wall-of-text density. It could still pass a single crowded paragraph with acceptable sentence lengths, which is a bad fit for Korean web-novel scanability and weakens retention stability.

Change: I tightened [`engine/prose_guard.py`](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/prose_guard.py) to measure paragraph sentence crowding, expose a `crowded_paragraph_ratio`, flag `crowded_paragraphs`, and penalize paragraphs that pack 5+ beats into one block. I added a regression case in [`tests/test_quality_gate.py`](/home/meta_os/web_novel/tests/test_quality_gate.py) proving the quality gate now rejects that failure mode while preserving existing readable-prose behavior.
