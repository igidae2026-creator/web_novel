**Improvement Bundle**

Bottleneck: the acceptance gate could still pass episodes with strong surface scores while the projected protagonist state had collapsed into pure backlash, which weakens persuasive protagonist fantasy and long-run character pressure.

Change: I added a direct `protagonist_momentum` gate in [engine/quality_gate.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/quality_gate.py#L36) and wired its threshold into [config.yaml](/home/meta_os/web_novel/config.yaml#L69). The check scores projected protagonist momentum from progress, backlash, urgency, decision pressure, and rival lead, then rejects episodes where pressure exists without enough countervailing agency. I also added regression coverage in [tests/test_quality_gate.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/tests/test_quality_gate.py#L612) and updated the end-to-end perception slice in [tests/test_top_tier_perception.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/tests/test_top_tier_perception.py#L116).
