**Bottleneck**

The highest-leverage bottleneck was the acceptance gate being too shallow. It could still pass episodes with decent surface scores while payoff setup/payoff integrity was drifting or the projected world state was becoming too unstable, which weakens payoff, coherence, and long-run reader trust.

**Change**

I tightened the runtime gate in [engine/quality_gate.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/quality_gate.py#L13) so it can enforce three projected-state checks in addition to the existing score/retention/ceiling/causality checks:

- `payoff_integrity_min`
- `payoff_corruption_flags_max`
- `world_instability_max`

The pipeline now passes projected story state into that gate at [engine/pipeline.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/pipeline.py#L455), so the rewrite/accept decision uses the actual next-state consequences, not just surface scoring. I activated the thresholds in [config.yaml](/home/meta_os/web_novel/config.yaml#L32), added regression coverage in [tests/test_quality_gate.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/tests/test_quality_gate.py#L6) and [tests/test_top_tier_perception.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/tests/test_top_tier_perception.py#L84), and recorded the new coverage in [docs/governance/COVERAGE_AUDIT.csv](/home/meta_os/web_novel/docs/governance/COVERAGE_AUDIT.csv#L30) plus the updated gap note in [docs/governance/WEBNOVEL_QUALITY_MAP.md](/home/meta_os/web_novel/docs/governance/WEBNOVEL_QUALITY_MAP.md#L40).
