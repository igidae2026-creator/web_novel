**Bottleneck**

The highest-leverage gap was in the repair path, not the gate itself. `engine/quality_gate.py` could already reject many serialization/coherence failures, but the rewrite prompt only gave targeted recovery instructions for a subset of those failure codes, which made retries depend too much on model luck.

**Change**

I replaced the partial hardcoded rewrite rules with a failure-code directive map in [engine/prompts.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/prompts.py#L6) and wired `episode_rewrite_json()` to emit the relevant repair instructions dynamically at [engine/prompts.py](/home/meta_os/web_novel/engine/prompts.py#L223). This now covers current gate failures like payoff integrity corruption, world instability, novelty drift, weak protagonist momentum, objective imbalance, weak cliffhangers, and cold threat proximity.
