# Agent Information Emotion

## Diagnosis

Information release and emotional buildup were mostly prompt-level, not stateful.

## Ceiling Bottleneck

Without tracked hidden truths and foreshadow queue, reveals are hard to pace and emotional payoff is unstable.

## Missing Architecture

- hidden truth ledger
- revealed truth ledger
- foreshadow queue
- dramatic irony level
- emotional reservoir

## Proposed Subsystem

`engine/information_emotion.py`

## Integration Points

- retention
- cliffhanger
- evaluation

## Regression Risks

- reveal spam
- too much hidden info without payoff

## Test Plan

- reveal events consume hidden truths
- dramatic irony remains measurable
